### üî¥ CRITICAL #1: NAS Vendor Lock-In

### üî¥ CRITICAL #2: Air-Gapped Networks

**Clinic Symptom:** "Credentials on USB, but no automated way to get them into vault when network still down"  
**Missing:** K4.5 Air-Gapped Offline Mode (ledger encryption + replay protocol)

**Problem:** OCT harvests 50 credentials, doesn't test any of them; 40% are expired/rotated

**Missing:** K4.9 Credential Validation Pipeline (test against actual services)  
**Effort:** 3-4 days

### üü† HIGH #4: Firmware-Locked Devices
**Problem:** CT/MRI workstations locked at OS level; cannot SSH/RDP; need serial console or IPMI  
**Clinic Symptom:** "Locked workstation has DICOM creds but we can't even boot it"  
**Missing:** K4.6 Hardware Access Module (serial, IPMI, boot interrupt)  
**Effort:** 4-5 days

### üü† HIGH #5: VLAN-Isolated Medical Devices
