üöÄ Emergency Protocols

Stroke Protocol

  1. Load head CT
  2. Press 3 for stroke preset
  3. Mouse wheel to scroll
  4. Look for hypodensity
  5. Press M to measure

Trauma Protocol

  1. Load trauma CT
  2. Press 4 (lung window)
  3. Press 7 (abdomen window)
  4. Press 9 (bone window)
  5. Space for cine mode

PE Protocol

  1. Load CTPA study
  2. Press 6 for PE preset
  3. Scroll through arteries
  4. Look for filling defects
  5. ROI tool to measure HU
