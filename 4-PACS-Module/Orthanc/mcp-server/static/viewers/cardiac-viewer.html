Cardiac Analysis Help

Analysis Types

  • Ejection Fraction: Measures heart pumping efficiency (normal: 50-70%)
  • Wall Motion: Analyzes regional cardiac wall movement
  • Chamber Volume: Calculates cardiac chamber volumes
  • Calcium Score: Quantifies coronary artery calcification

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Play/Pause animation
  • R - Reset view
  • W - Toggle wireframe
  • T - Toggle transparency
  • S - Take screenshot
  • 1-4 - Select analysis type

Wall Motion Scoring

  • Normal: Normal wall motion
  • Hypokinetic: Reduced wall motion
  • Akinetic: No wall motion
  • Dyskinetic: Paradoxical motion

Calcium Score Interpretation

  • 0: Very low risk
  • 1-10: Low risk
  • 11-100: Mild risk
  • 101-400: Moderate risk
  • >400: High risk
